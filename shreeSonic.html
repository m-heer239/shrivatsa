Plastic Granules

Here are the Plastic Granules we trade across India, offering a wide range of high-quality goods to meet diverse industry needs.

PP (Polypropylene)

Versatile and widely used for various applications, including packaging, textiles, automotive parts, and consumer goods. Known for its strength, durability, and resistance to chemicals.

HD (High-Density)

High-density polyethylene (HDPE) granules designed for injection molding applications. Ideal for creating strong, lightweight, and durable products such as containers, toys, and automotive components.

HD Pipe

HDPE granules specifically formulated for pipe manufacturing. These granules offer excellent resistance to corrosion, chemicals, and environmental stress, making them perfect for water, gas, and sewage pipes.

HD Blow

HDPE granules suitable for blow molding processes. Used to produce hollow plastic products like bottles, containers, and tanks, providing high impact strength and durability.

GPPS (General Purpose Polystyrene)

A transparent, rigid, and economical polymer used in a variety of applications such as packaging, disposable cutlery, and household items. It offers excellent clarity and ease of processing.

LD (Low-Density)

Low-density polyethylene (LDPE) granules used for making flexible and durable products like plastic bags, film wraps, and squeeze bottles. Known for its excellent resistance to moisture and chemicals.

PP Raffia (Raffia Grade Polypropylene)

Specifically designed for the production of raffia tapes, woven bags, and mats. This grade offers high tensile strength and durability, making it ideal for agricultural and industrial packaging.

PP Random

Random copolymer polypropylene used in applications requiring clarity and impact resistance. Commonly used in medical devices, transparent containers, and piping systems.

TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane)

A versatile polymer known for its elasticity, transparency, and resistance to oil, grease, and abrasion. Used in a variety of applications including footwear, flexible hoses, and automotive parts.

Plastic Granules

We supply all kinds of high-quality plastic granules used in various manufacturing processes, including polyethylene (PE), polypropylene (PP), LD (Low-Density), and more. Plastic Kitchenware Products

Our range of plastic kitchenware includes durable and stylish items such as containers, utensils, and storage solutions. We offer products that are BPA-free, food-grade certified, and designed to meet the highest standards of safety and convenience for everyday use.
